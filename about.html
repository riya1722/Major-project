About OrgoFarm

We are a family-run organic farm devoted to regenerative agriculture and honest produce.

Our Story

Founded in 2012 with a small plot and a big dream, OrgoFarm has grown into a farmer-led co-operative producing seasonal vegetables, fruits and organic staples. We partner with local communities for packing and training.

Our Practices

  • Composting & biofertilizers made on farm
  • Crop rotations & cover crops
  • Drip irrigation & rainwater harvesting
  • Beneficial insects & botanical pest control
